Our Story

The Beginning

Wicks & Giggles was born from a simple love for candles and the joy they bring to everyday moments. What started as a hobby in our small kitchen has now grown into a passion for creating handcrafted candles that light up your lives.

Our journey began in 2020 when our founder, Jennifer, decided to turn her candle-making hobby into something more. With a background in aromatherapy and a love for natural ingredients, she set out to create candles that not only smell amazing but also tell a story & hence hold's a memory.
